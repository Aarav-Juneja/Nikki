About Elikandle

At Elikandle, we believe in the power of nature to create harmony and balance in our lives. Our candles are handcrafted with love and care using natural ingredients to bring the beauty of nature into your home.

Each candle is meticulously crafted, combining the finest essential oils, soy wax, and organic materials to create a sensory experience that is both enchanting and comforting.

We are passionate about sustainability and creating products that are gentle on the environment. Our commitment to quality and eco-consciousness is reflected in every candle we make.

Discover the art of candlemaking and let the gentle glow and captivating aromas of Elikandle transform your space into a sanctuary of serenity.
